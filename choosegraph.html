Bar Chart

Bar chart or bar graph is a chart with rectangular bars with lengths proportional to the values that they represent

Scatter Plot

Scatter chart is a chart that displays data as a collection of points and shoes how much one variable is affected by another.

Stepped Area Chart

Step Area Charts are like line charts except that the area under them is shaded. It can be used to highlight the variation of values over time.

Line Interval Chart

Line intervals are used to depict the variance of multiple experiments and shows how a particular data changes at equal intervals of time.

Histogram

Histogram represents a frequency distribution by means of rectangles whose areas are proportional to the corresponding frequencies.

Bubble Chart

Bubble chart is a chart that displays three dimensions of data. An additional dimension of the data is represented in the size of the bubbles

Area Chart

Area charts are much like line charts, but they display different colours in the areas below the lines. The colourful and visual display distinguishes the data more clearly.

Combo Chart

Combo chart lets user display each data series as a different marker type, such as column, line, or area line.
